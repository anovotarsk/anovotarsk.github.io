Volunteer Work

Creating opportunities, comfort, and community for people who are navigating the same challenges I grew up with.

Free Programming Lessons for Students in Need

Programming Instructor · 2021 – 2022 · Self-organised

Designed free, flexible programming sessions in cafes, online meetings, and my home to teach C, C++, and Python to students who could not afford formal education.

  • Taught seven students computer literacy through data structures, pairing group workshops with 1:1 coaching.
  • Developed lessons, tasks, and review cycles that emphasised algorithmic thinking over rote memorisation.
  • Helped a student land a developer internship and empowered others to apply programming in creative work.
  • 7 students taught
  • 1 internship earned
  • 1.5 years of continuous lessons

Humanitarian Relief for Ukrainian Refugees

Volunteer Organiser & Founder · 2022 · Self-started

Launched a local initiative to serve families displaced by the full-scale invasion, focusing on fast, tangible relief.

  • Coordinated clothing and fund drives that gathered more than 200 kg of supplies for emergency shelters.
  • Upcycled donated foam into 100+ sleeping mats so refugees had clean, insulated bedding.
  • Partnered with shelters to deliver items where the need was greatest and tracked follow-up requests.
  • 200+ kg clothing & supplies delivered
  • 100+ sleeping mats crafted

Community Meetups for Displaced Ukrainians

Community Event Organiser · 2024 · Wrocław, Poland

Moved to Poland and co-founded free cultural and recreation events so fellow Ukrainians could find belonging in a new city.

  • Hosted social gatherings, holiday celebrations, and skill-sharing sessions for more than 100 people.
  • Curated partnerships with local venues to keep events accessible and family friendly.
  • Maintained on-going chat groups to help newcomers find housing, work leads, and emotional support.
  • 100+ participants welcomed
  • 9 months continuous meetups
