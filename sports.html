Rescue Sports & Adventure Tourism

Since 2015 I have been part of the Small Academy of Rescue Service, a regional branch of the nationwide youth movement “School of Safety.” In 2017 I became the team captain, leading training schedules, competition strategies, and mentorship for new members. I hold the First-Category (Adult) qualification in rescue sports, reflecting proficiency across rope work, technical rescue, and survival disciplines.

In 2020 the Federation of Sport Tourism of Ukraine certified me as an official referee, allowing me to organize and judge national-level events. As an athlete I have competed—and frequently medaled—in city, regional, and nationwide rescue competitions. Our team earned fourth place at the XV International Youth Fire Rescue Rally in Gomel, Belarus (2017), while I have also collected individual podiums in rope rescue, obstacle courses, and orienteering challenges.

Sports Tourism Volunteering

As a volunteer for youth sports tourism programs, I guide students through orienteering challenges, teamwork drills, and survival basics. This experience reinforces leadership skills and the ability to stay calm in dynamic environments.

Cycling & Strength Training

Cycling helps me maintain cardio fitness while balancing the demands of engineering work. I combine it with bodyweight and resistance training to stay injury-free and ready for long outdoor sessions.
